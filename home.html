About Us

 

SkyWand is an advanced aerial cinematography system that combines ease of use, repeatability, and precision. Harnessing cutting edge technologies in virtual reality (VR), animation, and drone flight, SkyWand allows users create and execute precise aerial filming plans that meet all cinematographic needs, without even flying the drone. The system removes technical barriers to achieving expert drone cinematography by automating the flight planning process, giving imaginative filmmakers a powerful partner in the sky. We are based in Atherton, CA.

We are looking for partnership opportunities with innovators, filmmakers, artists, and creatives to collaborate on projects that use the SkyWand technologies. We also work with commercial drone flight companies who want to explore new applications for drones.

Frequently Asked Questions

 

How do I get acquire a 3D model for the place I am planning to film?

You can either construct your own model by using photogrammetry, which includes structured fly scanning and 3D reconstruction software (like ContextCapture). Or you can integrate with 3rd party vendor like Google Maps, Apple Maps, or PLW for an pre-constructed model. Obviously they come with different price tags, and you should make your judgement based on your requirement. Photo comparison: Google/Photogrammetry/PLW/Real picture

Can I tune my trajectory during flights?

Yes, our IPad app, browser tool and Maya plugin all support editing your trajectory anywhere, the only requirement is that you will need to connect to the Internet for uploading.

How fast can the drone move when program is controlling it?

You can push to the full limit of the drone. 15m/s for Inspire 1.

Does wind affect the drone flight trajectory?

Yes, calmer weather will give you a smoother shot. In our experience when wind speed exceeds 10m/s the shot become unusable.

Is there a limit on the number of key frames?

In current release we do have a limit of 50 key frames. We are planning to raise this limit in future release.

What features do you have for drone safety?

Continue to follow trajectory even when RC controller signals is lost. One button to abort mission and stay still. Control the flying speed along the trajectory by pushing on the joystick, or pull back to make the drone reverse the trajectory.

What feature do you have for the VR editor?

Simulate real camera focal length and zoom. Simulate realistic sun/moon movement and lighting base on the GPS location in the model. Preview and adjustment.

What camera do you support?

We provide camera support through our vendors' drone platform. Please consult our supported drone vendors' website (DJI, Freefly System) for detail information. Currently the best camera DJI Inspire 1 can carry is a Micro 4/3, and on Freefly System that's a RED.

Can I get a customized version of your software?

Absolutely, we can customize the belt of technologies for your requirement. Please send us an email at botao@skywand.com.
